About The Keeper of Shadows

I'm Supreet Vishnu, and my life? Well... it's kind of like living in a haunted mansion. Shadows dance in the corners of my mind, strange stories echo through the halls, and you'll always find a little chill in the air around me — but that's just how I like it.

I'm a writer who's obsessed with the strange and the spooky. I love creating stories that make your skin crawl and your heart race — tales where nothing is quite what it seems and even the bravest might hesitate to turn the page.

My Creative Philosophy

"True horror isn't about the monster under the bed — it's about the realization that you were never alone in the room to begin with."
